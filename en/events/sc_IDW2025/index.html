SciDataCon 2025 | International Data Week 2025

SciDataCon 2025 is an international conference dedicated to advancing the frontiers of data in research and policy. Held as part of International Data Week 2025 (IDW 2025), the event brings together scientists, data professionals, policymakers, and technologists to discuss how open, interoperable, and well-managed data can drive discovery, innovation, and equitable development.

It is organized by the International Science Council (ISC)'s two data bodies: CODATA (the ISC's Committee on Data) and the World Data System (WDS).
