## Introduction

The three-day workshop is being organized by the Bioinformatics Infrastructure Facility (BIF) in collaboration with the Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Center for Biomedical Research (ACBR), University of Delhi, with support from a Department of Biotechnology (DBT)–funded project. 

The program will comprise expert lectures and hands-on training sessions designed for faculty members, researchers, and undergraduate and postgraduate students. It will focus on the application of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and core bioinformatics methodologies in the domain of drug discovery.

The workshop will be conducted in offline mode from 15–17 January 2026 at ACBR, University of Delhi.
