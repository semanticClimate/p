Open Science Fair 2025 | Accelerating Open Science through Collaboration

The Open Science Fair 2025 (OSFair 2025) conference is co-organized by CERN’s Open Science Section and OpenAIRE to emphasize the importance of global, international, and cross-regional partnerships in advancing Open Science.

The aim of the event is to bring together the diverse group of collaborations across the globe to accelerate knowledge discovery, accessibility for creating more transparent and equitable research ecosystem.

Theme: Fusing Forces – Accelerating Open Science through Collaboration

Date

  • September 15 - 17, 2025

Location
