OT-ViRARe aims to move beyond traditional publication-centric and metric-driven evaluation systems by developing transparent, equitable, and AI-ready approaches that recognize the full diversity of research contributions across disciplines, institutions, projects, and regions.

## Why OT-ViRARe?

Research assessment systems worldwide continue to rely heavily on journal-based metrics and citation indicators. While useful in some contexts, these measures often fail to capture:

- Open Science contributions
- Research data and software outputs
- Interdisciplinary collaboration
- Community engagement and societal impact
- FAIR data practices
- Contributions from the Global South

OT-ViRARe addresses these challenges by integrating semantic technologies, knowledge graphs, AI-assisted analysis, and data visitation approaches to support more inclusive and context-aware assessment frameworks.

This initiative builds upon experiences gained through the #semanticClimate project, extending semantic AI approaches beyond climate-related documents to support assessment of:

- Research organizations
- Research projects
- Scientific themes
- Research infrastructures
- Open Science activities

### Our Vision

To build a global ecosystem of open, interoperable, and trustworthy tools that enable responsible research assessment while respecting data sovereignty, privacy, and local contexts.

#### Planned Activities and Outputs (2025–2027)

#### 2025 

- Core Team Establishment
- ECR  Engagement

#### 2026

- Release of ORAT v1.0 (Open Research Assessment Toolkit)
- Guidelines for visitation-enabled responsible research assessment
- Launch of TG Working Groups on metadata/ontologies, policy frameworks, and capacity-building
- Metadata & Ontology Toolkit for multilingual, culturally sensitive, visitation-based semantic enrichment
- Regional workshops across Asia, Africa, and Latin America

#### 2026

- OT-ViRARe White Paper
- Joint symposium with CODATA, RDA, GOSC, DSJ and national committees on integrating visitation into RRA
- Final report and open repositories of tools, schemas, demonstrators, training materials
- Presentation of outputs to the CODATA Community
