Delhi Research Implementation and Innovation (DRIIV)

Delhi Research Implementation and Innovation (DRIIV), addresses educational, environmental and societal problems pertinent to Delhi and neighbouring regions; imncluding but not limited to Effective Education, Solid Waste Management, Water Security, Air Pollution, application of AI/ML in Healthcare and Sustainable Mobility.

Delhi Effective Education and Pedagogy Cluster (DEEP-C)

One part of DRIIV is the Delhi Effective Education and Pedagogy Cluster (DEEP-C) where we aim to improve learners' competencies in analytical, critical thinking, problem-solving, and inquiry skills and introduce students/trainers to the current fields of research and innovation to enhance their employability skills in the long run.
