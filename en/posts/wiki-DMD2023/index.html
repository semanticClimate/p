Wikidata : Data Modelling Days 2023

The Data Modelling Days 2023 took place as a virtual event tailored for the Wikidata community, concentrating on the intricacies of describing and structuring data on Wikidata.

Event schedule

30 November - 2 December 2023

Event Report

Dr. Gitanjali Yadav, Founder #semanticClimate, has presented the work entitled "Building a semantic knowledge (wiki)base for synthetic phytochemistry" in the session Building and modelling a Wikibase instance from scratch on 2 December.
