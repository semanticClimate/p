CBC 2025 | Annual Computational Biology Conference 2025

CBC 2025 is an annual event organized by Indian Biological Data Centre (IBDC), Regional Centre For Biotechnology (RCB), Faridabad, India. This event brings together the researchers, data scientists, developers and experts from computational biology to discuss their research and exchange insights.
